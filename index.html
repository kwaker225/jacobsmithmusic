About

Just Him & the Guitar

Based in Milford, Michigan, Jacob Smith grew up in the small town of Byron, MI where music was always close by. He plays entirely acoustic — no backing tracks, no band, just a guitar and a room full of people. His sets move between original songs and covers that span rock, country, and folk, built for breweries, taprooms, and anywhere else people want music they can talk over, sing along to, or just sit with for a while.
